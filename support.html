Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my subscription plan?

You can change your plan by navigating to the Billing section via the avatar dropdown menu. Click on "Change Plan" to see available options. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

How do I add a new staff member?

Currently, staff management is found in the main sidebar under "Team". You can invite staff via their email and assign them to specific services and working hours.

My dashboard numbers aren't updating live?

The dashboard auto-refreshes every 5 minutes. If you need to see immediate changes after a booking, simply refresh the page. Pull-to-refresh will be available in the upcoming mobile update.

Can I export my client list?

Yes, under the "Customers" tab in the sidebar, there is an "Export to CSV" button in the top right corner. This export includes basic contact details and total visit counts.
